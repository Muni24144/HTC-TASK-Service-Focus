Modules

Incident Management

Includes the capability to identify, record, categorize, and prioritize incidents, leading to improved service efficiency and productivity.

Change Management

Robust tools efficiently help your change team collaborate and manage change processes in order to minimize risks and service disruptions.

Knowledge Management

Includes a powerful AI-based search That is integrated with conversational intelligence to understand the intent and auto-suggest the right KB article that will help agents and users to resolve issues quicker

Problem Management

Helps to identify the root cause, prevent recurrence and determine a permanent fix, thereby increasing productivity, and improving service quality and customer experience.

Self Service Portal

Leverages a powerful form builder tool and workflow engine that can be utilized to design forms and publish them quickly.

Safe and Secure

Solution utilizes Multi-Factor Authentication AD and Social Media Authentication Single Sign-On features.

Reimagine Self-Service with Intelligent Knowledge Base

A powerful AI-based search which is integrated with conversational intelligence that reimagines self-service. Intelligent knowledge base that goes beyond traditional approach which empowers users to find the answers they need.

Automate Repetitive Tasks

ServiceFocus is an intelligent solution that understands end user intent, routes tickets, and aids in ticket resolution. Ticket creation is done via email, with templated responses. Automated Event Monitoring is utilized for SLA tracking, Missed FLR, and High Ticket Tracking situations.

Make Better Decisions based on Data

ServiceFocus provides real-time Metric Dashboards where call, QA, ticketing, and performance metrics can be viewed. Call volumes can also be forecasted, and workforce can be predicted. Collaborations can take place via Live Presence, Live Chat, Kanban Boards, posts, and discussion threads.
